Why Genius

The best English medium school with all facilities for students' mental, physical, and cultural progress. A campus very close to Kolhapur city but free from both noise and air pollution, set in nature. Spacious and well-equipped classrooms. Residential facilities are available. Well-educated teachers and experienced administrative staff ensure the efficient running of the school. Facilities for sports include qualified and experienced physical instructors.

The school building is highly secure and equipped with advanced classrooms, an activity room, a computer lab, a library, and a vast playground. The students get opportunities to prove their talent through various competitive exams and extra-curricular activities. The teachers take the onus of devising new techniques so that learning becomes simple and interesting for the students. The security and maintenance of the school are perfectly managed by the authorities.

With LEAD’s school management system, students get the privilege to attend video-enabled classes, learning programs, webinars, and e-workshops. Admission of candidates is based on the result of their performance in a written test and interview.
